- **Visual design**: making your page sexy! = graphic elements, colors, typography, layout, ... - **Functional design**: responding to your users' needs. = navigation system, menus, buttons, links, ...
*« Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works. »* - Steve Jobs -- Working on web-design is thinking of: - Visual design, - Experience, - Accessibility
The User is central. That is why we call that way of thinking design the [User-centered Design](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/User-centered_design). -- ## Visual design **The feeling you want your user to have.** Your tools are : - colors (harmony, dark, ...), - typographies (hand-written, average, ...), - page layout (vertical, messy, ...), - shapes (round, square, ...), - logos and illustrations, - pictures, videos and sounds. -- ## Experience (UI / UX) Knowing your user and letting him understand your message. Your tools are:
  • Persona,
  • Scenario,
  • User tests.
-- ## Accessibility Making your website visible by anyone. - Responsive - Understandable by disabled people
Some [best pratices](https://www.webaccessibility.com/best_practices.php).
